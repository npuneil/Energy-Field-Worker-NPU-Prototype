FIRST TIME SETUP
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  1. Double-click: Setup.bat  (or run Setup.ps1 in PowerShell)
  2. Wait for dependencies to install (~1-2 minutes)
  3. Double-click: StartApp.bat
  4. App opens in your browser at http://localhost:5000

DEMO FEATURES (4 Tabs)
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  TAB 1 – HOME
  • Overview of on-device AI capabilities for Zava Energy field operations
  • Value propositions: $0 inference, data sovereignty, offline/remote

  TAB 2 – FIELD INSPECTION AI
  • Upload/describe pipeline, facility, or wellsite conditions
  • Get instant AI hazard assessment, risk level, recommended actions
  • Sample scenarios included for easy demo
  • Voice dictation for hands-free field reporting

  TAB 3 – OPERATIONS ASSISTANT
  • Chat with AI about safety procedures, regulations, field ops
  • Pre-built quick prompts for common questions (CSA Z662, H2S, etc.)
  • All conversation processed on-device

  TAB 4 – DOCUMENT ANALYZER
  • Paste inspection reports, permits, SOPs, or regulatory docs
  • AI summarizes, extracts data, or flags compliance issues
  • Sample pipeline integrity report included
